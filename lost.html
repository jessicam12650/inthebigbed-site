How Find my dog works

Step 01
Tap the alert button
One tap. No long forms. Just your dog's name and last known location.
Step 02
Your community is notified
Every In The Big Bed user within 2 miles gets an instant push notification with your dog's photo and description.
Step 03
Sightings come in
Users who spot your dog tap "I've seen them" and their location is pinged straight to you.
Step 04
You find them
Follow the sightings in real time on a live map until you're reunited.

Built by someone who knows.

The founder of In The Big Bed lost their dog a few years ago. They know the terror of that moment — the frantic searching, the helplessness, the desperate hope that someone somewhere has seen them. Find my dog exists because no owner should go through that alone. The whole Liverpool dog community is your search party.

Every alert is anonymous to the public. Your details are only shared with verified users who report a sighting.
